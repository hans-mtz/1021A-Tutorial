Recap

  • Relaionship between two vars:

    • Positive: Move in same direction (upward slope)
    • Negative: Move in opposite directions (downward slope)

  • Given two points (\(x_1, y_1\)) and (\(x_2,y_2\)),

    • the slope is \(\frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x} = \frac{y_2-y_1}{x_2-x_1}\)
